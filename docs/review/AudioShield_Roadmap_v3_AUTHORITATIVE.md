# AudioShield — Roadmap v3 (Authoritative)
*Supersedes AudioShield_PostAudit_Roadmap_v2 / v2_1 in full. Reconciles three collaborator documents, which are the governing authorities in case of any conflict with this file: (1) AI_VOICE_DETECTION_RESEARCH_AUDIT.md ("the audit"), (2) Honest_verdict.docx ("HV" — the collaborator's review of Roadmap v2), (3) code_issue.md ("CI" — the adaptive test-leakage evidence chain). Self-contained: a fresh session with only these documents can execute this plan.*

**End goal (anchor for every decision):** strong, defensible novelty + real SOTA competitiveness + a submittable top-tier paper. Per HV's venue assessment, "top-tier" is operationalized honestly: **a strong Interspeech/ICASSP submission (HV's estimate: 45–65% acceptance if well-executed)**, with ACL/AAAI as a stretch target only if the reliance principle demonstrably transfers beyond one architecture. NeurIPS/ICLR-class venues are explicitly out of scope for this cycle per both the audit (§12) and HV — pretending otherwise would be the "diluted or rushed" version of the goal, not the strong one.

**The research question (HV's framing, adopted verbatim as the paper's spine):** *What representation-level measurement actually distinguishes harmless domain encoding from decision-relevant domain dependence in audio deepfake detectors?* Three preregistered outcomes, all of which are legitimate papers: strong success (diagnostic + regularizer win → full ICASSP/Interspeech paper), diagnostic-only success (analysis/toolkit paper), failure (pivot to replay/media robustness on the repaired infrastructure).

## 0. What changed from v2 (delta log — HV/CI item → change)

1. **CI (adaptive test leakage proven):** ITW/ReplayDF/AI4T are formally reclassified as *development OOD diagnostic corpora* everywhere (code, docs, paper). The e005-C → e007 `spoof_pos_weight` chain is disclosed, not hidden; e007 A/B/C are acknowledged as downstream of a test-influenced hyperparameter (the A-vs-B *within-pair* comparison survives — both arms use 1.0 — but its interpretation carries the disclosure).
2. **HV (gradient-score flaw):** the single gradient DRS is demoted. For linear heads it reduces to constant weight–subspace alignment and must not be called "causal reliance." Replaced by a **battery of complementary reliance measures** (see Step 3), including HV's variance-weighted form R_var = (wᵀUUᵀΣ_zUUᵀw)/(wᵀΣ_zw), class-conditional and out-of-fold.
3. **HV (factor-specific reliance):** one generic "domain" subspace is replaced by factor-specific subspaces — U_generator, U_codec, U_channel, U_language, U_speaker/source, U_corpus — with the falsifiable prediction *reliance on factor X predicts failure under shift of factor X*. The potential headline finding is factor asymmetry (e.g., codec reliance harmful; some channel information necessary for calibration; full invariance suboptimal).
4. **HV (novelty positioning):** TCAV / INLP / LEACE / KRaM / FastCAV are acknowledged prior art; "decodability ≠ reliance" is not novel as a concept. Novelty must come from the three-part empirical result (diagnostic validity + causal validity + optimization value) in the deepfake-detection setting, across factors and backbones. **LEACE, INLP, and TCAV become direct baselines**, not just citations.
5. **HV (gate preregistration):** Step 4's pass/fail criteria are now quantitative and committed to git *before* the gate runs (list in Step 4).
6. **HV (interventions):** embedding-space removal/swap alone is insufficient. The battery adds **waveform-level paired counterfactuals** (same audio through codecs/channels/replay/resampling, dose–response), matched swaps, manifold-plausibility checks, and effects on logits *and calibrated probabilities*, not only EER.
7. **HV (repair split):** Step 2 splits into correctness-critical fixes (before anything else) vs. convenience refactors (deferred until after baseline reproduction) — no big refactor before results are reproduced.
8. **HV (staged compute):** the v2 "750 GPU-h" plan is replaced by staged elimination: 1 seed → 3 seeds → 5 seeds for shortlisted conditions only.
9. **HV (blindness):** public sets (XMAD-Bench, ML-ITW) are not inherently blind. Blindness = frozen repo commit + preregistered analysis plan *before* first contact, or a hidden/challenge-server split.
10. **HV (second backbone):** mandatory. All principal claims demonstrated on ≥2 SSL backbones (XLS-R-300M + WavLM-Large).
11. **HV (parallelism):** dataset acquisition is explicitly *not* a hard blocker; it proceeds in parallel with validity work. (v2 already ran it in parallel; v3 removes any gate language implying otherwise.)

## 1. Steps (gated order; ∥ = parallel-safe)

**STEP 1 — Phase-0 sync (collaborator machine; runbook exists; unchanged mechanics, expanded content).**
Push/merge/tag the true e007 state; result bundles + checkpoint hashes; checksum verification. v3 additions to the same commit series: (a) RESULTS.md/README language revision — remove "breakthrough / regressed / trade-off / target model" framing (CI §4 uses this wording as leakage evidence); neutral descriptions + one explicit disclosure paragraph documenting the spoof_pos_weight chain and the reclassification of ITW/ReplayDF/AI4T as development corpora; (b) commit CI and HV into the repo's `docs/` as governing review records. *From: CI (full chain); audit §5, P0.5–P0.6.*

**STEP 2 — Repair, split in two.**
**2a (correctness-critical, one PR, before anything downstream):** seeding plumbed everywhere + logged; sampler class×corpus factorization + VCTK handling (matched synthetic or exclusion from class-conditional objectives); loader fail-fast; dependency/HF-revision pinning + lockfile; probe protocol (grouped folds, balanced accuracy, honest chance); EER single-sort; SupCon guard; manifest schema (source_id, speaker_id, generator_id, channel_id, platform_id, attack). **Reproduce e005-A and e007-A/B headline numbers under the repaired stack before proceeding** — divergence beyond tolerance is investigated, not papered over. **2b (convenience, deferred until after Step 4):** entry-point consolidation, docs links, path hygiene, refactors. *From: audit §4.2/§4.4/§4.7/§5; HV "split the large repair phase."*

**STEP 3 — Reliance instrumentation (replaces "DRS rebuild").**
Per checkpoint, per **factor** (generator, codec, channel, language, speaker/source, corpus), in that checkpoint's own coordinates, class-controlled, with **nested cross-fitting** (data selecting subspace rank/estimator/intervention strength never evaluates effects): report the complementary battery — (i) geometric weight–subspace alignment (named as such, never "causal"); (ii) **R_var** variance-weighted reliance (HV's formula), class-conditional, out-of-fold; (iii) prediction change under projection removal and under **LEACE**; (iv) performance change under matched counterfactuals; (v) conditional-MI / nonlinear predictability where practical. Two subspace estimators throughout (e.g., class-conditional LDA directions vs. cross-fitted linear-probe weights); grouped-bootstrap uncertainty; rank-sensitivity curves. LEACE/INLP implementations built here serve double duty as Step-5/6 baselines. *From: audit §3/§8-A; HV "gradient score" + "report several complementary quantities" + "nested cross-fitting."*

**STEP 4 — THE GATE (decisive research experiment; CPU + cached embeddings + cheap seeded head replicates; preregistered).**
Before running, commit `docs/gate_prereg.md` containing the quantitative pass criteria, verbatim from HV: replication across ≥2 backbones; positive reliance↔matching-factor-degradation association across checkpoints; grouped-bootstrap CIs; intervention effects significantly exceeding equal-norm random controls (with task-direction removal as positive control); rank stability; agreement across ≥2 subspace estimators; no collapse after controlling for clean EER / checkpoint quality / training corpus; consistent effect direction across ≥3 independently seeded replicates. The seeded replicates and the second backbone are affordable inside the gate: heads retrain on cached embeddings in minutes (3 seeds × conditions), and a WavLM-Large embedding pass over locally present corpora (eval trio, FoR, MLAAD re-fetch subset) plus the collaborator machine's legacy corpora provides backbone #2 without new training. The intervention battery includes the **waveform-level paired counterfactuals** (codec chains, RIR/replay simulation, resampling; dose–response; using locally present raw audio + the manifest re-fetch subset), manifold-plausibility checks (nearest-neighbor/reconstruction), and logits + calibrated probabilities. **Outcomes:** strong success → Step 6 includes the reliance regularizer; diagnostic-only → Step 6 becomes the analysis/toolkit paper track (measurement release, factor-asymmetry findings); failure → pivot to replay/media robustness (bar: beat W2V2-AASIST 18.2% / adaptive-RIR 11.0% on the official ReplayDF protocol) on the repaired infrastructure. *From: audit §8-B/P2; HV "preregistered gates" + "three legitimate outcomes" + "intervention controls."*

**STEP 5 ∥ — Trustworthy baselines under one protocol (starts after 2a; overlaps Step 4).**
Frozen-SSL + LR reproduction (Pascu-style), W2V2-AASIST + RawBoost/RIR, data-centric mixture baseline (the Stage-1 mix sweep + pruning slot here), calibration operations (dev-fitted; Brier/NLL/Cllr/actDCF/ECE; selective risk). Baseline set for the method comparison now includes **LEACE, INLP, TCAV-style sensitivity control** alongside GRL/CDANN, CORAL/MMD, GroupDRO, SAM, class-conditional SupCon. Evaluation-tier policy in force: development tier = ITW/ReplayDF/AI4T (+ pool_v2 holdouts as validation); **final tier untouched and governed by the blindness protocol** — repository commit + analysis plan frozen and tagged before first contact with XMAD-Bench / ML-ITW / temporal holdout; no iteration afterward. *From: audit §4.1/§4.3/§4.10/§9; HV "not inherently blind" + "LEACE/INLP/TCAV as direct baselines."*

**STEP 6 — Staged final training (only per Step-4 outcome; both backbones).**
Stage A: 1-seed elimination across candidate conditions (CE, SupCon, GRL, CORAL or GroupDRO, SAM, LEACE-erasure-trained, reliance-regularized if gated in). Stage B: 3-seed confirmation of survivors. Stage C: 5-seed finals for the shortlisted 3–4 conditions, pre-registered selection metric, dev-tier tuning only, code frozen per stage. Both backbones for principal comparisons. Budget is spent only where Stage A shows signal. *From: audit P3/§9; HV "stage the compute budget" + "second backbone."*

**STEP 7 — Blind evaluation + release.**
Single-shot on the frozen final tier under the preregistered analysis plan; factorized reporting (per generator/codec/channel/language + compound + temporal); full release: code tag, lockfile, manifests + hashes, result bundles, the reliance-measurement toolkit (this is the publishable artifact of the diagnostic-only branch), preserved negatives. *From: audit §4.1/P4; HV venue checklist.*
